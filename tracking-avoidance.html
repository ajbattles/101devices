Tracking & Avoidance

Device Information

The tracking and avoidance sensors do the same thing - detect if something is in front of the sensor. It does this by emitting infrared and looking if it bounces back. Not all materials reflect infrared, some will absorb it and appear invisible to the sensor. The range of these devices are only a few cm.

Applications

  • Robot manuvering
  • Barcode scanner (black lines will absorb IR)
