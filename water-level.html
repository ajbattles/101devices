Water Level

Device Information

The water level sensor can be used to detect the level of water. This works because the water changes the resistance of the device as it rises. A high water level would cause the device to have low resistance. These sensors do not have a long lifespan - water degrades it quickly. The lifespan of the device can be increased by only powering the device to take readings. If it is always powered, it will degrade faster.

Applications

  • Used in sump pits to know when to run pump (Civil Engineering)
  • Water level alarm - detects spills, or if tank is near capacity
