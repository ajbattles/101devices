Temperature Sensors

Device Information

The DS18B20, KY-208, and DHT11 all measure temperature. The DHT11 also measures humidity. The electric behavior of semiconductors changes with temperature, allowing measurements to be made

Applications

  • Graphic card fans - prevents overheating
  • Autoclaves - cleaning medical tools, HVAC Control
