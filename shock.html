KY-002 Shock Sensor

Device Information

This devices provides an output if a shock has been detected. There are a few designs for a shock sensor. The fundamental principle behind shock senors is to have two metal pieces of metal connect upon the device having a shock - allowing voltage to be shared. One way to do this is with a spring surrounding a pin - if the spring receives a great enough shock, it will bend and touch the pin.

Applications

  • HDD protection on laptops - stops the disk from reading or writing if a shock is detected from a drop
  • Monitoring fragile shipments
  • Airbag sensors
  • Fall sensors for senior citizens
  • Emergency locator beacons
