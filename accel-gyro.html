GY-521 Accelerometer & Gyroscope

Device Information

This device measures accelerations. The accelerometer measures acceleration in one direction, while the gyroscope measures angular acceleration in one axis. Using integration, we can use this to determine the orientation, speed, and position of the object - though you will need to have a way to correct the mathematical error that will arise over time.

Applications

  • Smart phones have an accelerometer & gyroscope that applications can get data from
  • Mechanical Engineers can use this for robot & vehicle stabilization
  • Rockets use internal guidance systems (IGS) to keep track of its location while accelerating
