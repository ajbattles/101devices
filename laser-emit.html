KY-008 Laser Emit

Device Information

This device can emit a red laser when powered. This device is supposed to be used in conjuction with other devices such as the photoresistor. While this laser is not so powerful, powerful lasers in engineering have important uses.

Applications

  • Laser Welder
  • Laser Printing
  • LIDAR (measuring distances)
  • Garage Doors
